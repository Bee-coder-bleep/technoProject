Get To Know Us

About Us

Techno-Plast Ltd was established in October 1994 and has become one of the leading plastic water tank manufacturers in Kenya. Over the years the Techno-Plast Ltd. has perfected the art of roto molding. We manufacture the best quality tanks from 100% virgin material giving the longest possible life span. We do not compromise on quality and are always focused on customer satisfaction. All our tanks have an inner layer of special UV stabilized material giving the tank anti-microbial properties.

Delivery

We have a fleet of well-maintained vehicles that are always ready to deliver our products countrywide within a few days of order! This is to allow us to process your order and give time for production of your goods. Once your order and payment have been processed, a member of our dispatch team will get in touch with you to let you know exactly when your tanks will be dispatched.
